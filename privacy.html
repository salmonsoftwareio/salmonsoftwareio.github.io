Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications developed by Salmon Software (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") that are published as Ad Supported services and do not require user registration or collect personal data.

If an individual app under our brand deviates from this policy (e.g., by collecting user information, offering user accounts, or enabling cloud saves), a separate privacy policy will be provided specific to that app.

Information Collection and Use

These applications do not collect personal information when you download or use them. No registration is required to use any of these applications.

Our applications do not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Since we do not collect user data, no data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not used to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. We encourage all children to never submit any personally identifiable information through our applications.

Parents and legal guardians should monitor their children's internet usage and help enforce this Policy. You must be at least 16 years of age to consent to any data processing in your country (in some countries, a parent or guardian may consent on your behalf).

Security & Opt-Out Rights

The Service Provider is committed to safeguarding your privacy. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of your data being accessed by unauthorized individuals.

You can stop all use of our applications by uninstalling them using the standard uninstall process on your device or app store.

Changes & Contact
